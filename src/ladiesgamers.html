LadiesGamers

Design, Development, Writing - 2020

LadiesGamers is a gaming review website, that is all about Indie and wholesome gaming. Their writers and readers are of different genders and ages, and they write from a family friendly perspective. They promote and review indie games, which may not receive mainstream coverage and specialize in wholesome games.

Client: LadiesGamers
Website: www.ladiesgamers.com

LadiesGamers content

I have been working at LadiesGamers for a couple of years now. The website is all about reviewing laid-back handheld gaming. I have always loved to play video games so this is a fun creative outlet for me. While my main job is to design a website banner every season and I am also in charge of illustrating Patreon backgrounds. Over the years I have become more and more involved on the website by also writing reviews on games. More recently I have been helping out with the styling of the wordpress.
