Strijp-T

Concepting, Design, Developer - 2020

Strijp-T is an innovation district in Eindhoven. The focus is on sustainable business in an inspiring and dynamic environment. Many high-tech companies are located on Strijp-T. Fontys ICT and the Delta can also be found here. Our task in this project was to further strengthen the identity of Strijp-T and all its residents.

Client: Strijp-T
Website: www.strijp-t.nl

Strijp-T Hotspot App

As a group we made a concept for an app that has several hotspots in the Strijp-T area and these locations can be found by visitors. When the visitors arrive at the hotspot, they can scan a QR-code and the Strijp-T app will open. At these hotspots visitors are presented with videos of old employees from Philips and they see an overview of companies, located near that specific hotspot.

My part in designing and realizing this app are the pages that are displayed when the user is at a hotspot. On the first page there is the video, some information about the hotspot and a couple of recommended businesses, that can be found near that specific hotspot. The second page shows a full list of companies at Stijp-T and their location.
For the development part of the app, we opted for a Progressive Web App because this way, all platforms (like: IOS and Android) could be reached. The actual coding is being done with vue.js framework, in which we are making templates of each page.
