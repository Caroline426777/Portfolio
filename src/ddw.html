Dutch Design Week

Concepting, Design, Development - 2022

Every year the Dutch Design Week takes place in Eindhoven. This is a big design event where designers present their work and ideas. DDW focuses mainly on the future of design and promotes young talents who want to get involved here. The focus of the DDW event is on experimentation, innovation, and crossover. For this yearâ€™s Dutch Design Week, Fontys offers its visitors an immersive and personalized, data-driven experience in their ICT InnovationLab.

Client: Fontys ICT

Concepting

For this project, we Deltas had the task of brainstorming and coming up with an idea for DDW's immersive building experience. The main theme was interactive building and we had to come up with concepts that had to do with emotions and/or memories.
After many iterations, I combined two of my concepts. The first concept was about the building seeing you as a building material, because the building itself is made of different materials. The visitor's face is scanned, and the program renders their face with different materials and textures. In the second concept, the building sees you as body heat. It reads your emotions and shows you where the hot and cold spots are based on that.

The final product is a combination of the two concepts explained above. The building sees the visitor's body as different building materials and shows the visitor a mirror in which the person is a material.
