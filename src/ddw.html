Dutch Design Week

Concepting, Design, Developer - 2022

Interactive Ellie is an initiative of Fontys ICT. A couple of years back they decided to break down one of the school buildings to rebuild a new one. The Delta group thought it fun to make the to be broken down building come to life. Similar to the car from Christine. The purpose over the years for this project is to make the building come alive. We did this by designing Ellie's Secret Agenda.

Client: Fontys ICT
