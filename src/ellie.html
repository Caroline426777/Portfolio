Interactive Ellie

Concepting, Design, Developer - 2020

Interactive Ellie is an initiative of Fontys ICT. A couple of years back they decided to break down one of the school buildings to rebuild a new one. The Delta group thought it fun to make the to be broken down building come to life. Similar to the car from Christine. The purpose over the years for this project is to make the building come alive. We did this by designing Ellie's Secret Agenda.

Client: Fontys ICT

Interactive Ellie App

With the app we want students to be able to carry Ellie with them and feel like they are a part of Ellie. As the name suggests it is an agenda of sorts, but it goes way beyond a standard planner. In this agenda you can see your daily tasks, meetings, workshops that apply to you and keeps track of how for you have come in completing your learning outcomes. Ellie also gives you tips throughout the day, some tips could be: A new command that you can try at the Ellie map, where you can buy lunch, what the weather is like and how to enjoy or avoid it, etc. With this app, Ellie is trying to give you the best school day possible.
